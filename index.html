Guorui Zhong

Research Fellow

Bioinformatics Institute, A*STAR

Biography

I am a postdoctoral research fellow (Mentor: Dr. Loo Lit-Hsin) at Bioinformatics Institute (A*STAR), working primarily on investigating the modes of action of xenobiotics (eg., environmental agents and drugs), and ultimaltely developing human disease related in vitro models that can predict their in vivo effects of these agents.

Interests
  • CRISPR/Cas gene editing
  • HTC imaging and analysis
  • R data analysis and visualization
  • Chemical hazard assessment
  • Assay/Model development
  • Toxicology & Immunology
